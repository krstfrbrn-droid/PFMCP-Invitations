We commend the time and effort you invested in this training program to enhance your public financial management competency skills. We hope that you will continue your Public Financial Management Competency Program (PFMCP) journey. If you wish to proceed, please be informed of the next steps below.

Kindly refer to the complete details on the PFMCP provided below for your guidance.

The Public Financial Management Competency Program (PFMCP) is designed to enhance the PFM-relevant skills and functions of the Philippine PFM workforce as well as further strengthen the PFM capacity-building program, as part of the PFM Reform Roadmap.

  • 1
    Foundation Track (FT)
  • 2
    Budgeting and Performance Track (BPT)
    • Introduction to Budgeting (ITB) module
    • Fiscal Planning (FP) module
    • Budget Preparation (BP)
    • Budget Execution (BE) module
    • Developments in Budgeting (DB) module
  • 3
    Internal Audit Track for Non-Internal Audit Practitioners (IAT)
  • ◆Personnel with Salary Grades 11 to 22 (strictly for technical staff only)
  • ◆Personnel who perform key PFM functions: Budgeting, Planning, Accounting, Auditing, Procurement, Cash Management
  1. Foundation Track is a prerequisite course before taking any technical tracks (BPT and IAT).
  2. Each agency is given a maximum of 5 slots per BPT cycle. Enrollment is on a first-come, first-served basis.
  3. Participants must be endorsed by their Bureau/Service/Office Head or authorized representative.
  4. For first-time/new enrollees of BPT, participants are required to finish one (1) full cycle under a block section, taking modules in proper sequence.
  5. Previous BPT enrollees (2025 and below) are encouraged to finish remaining modules and may enroll on any available dates.

The partner agency shall express their interest to enroll in the PFMCP through the submission of the Participant Nomination Form to: 📧 bitscdd@dbm.gov.ph

The form must be approved and copy furnished to the authorized representative.
